A Little Information About Me


For the first 15 years of my career I worked in the hotel business and the last 11 years I have been selling to hotels. While working for a hotel company, I taught myself Visual Basic (for DOS!) in order to write an employee speakout program. It was a complex program for a first try, but I was hooked. I started my own website design company in 2000 using primarily html and Microsoft Frontpage. A few months ago I decided to go back to school to get some coding education and I have loved almost every minute of it. Now I want to use my extensive background in sales and marketing with my new found knowledge of full stack development.


Where I Have Worked

Hospitality Partners - For 15 years I worked in almost every position in a hotel - front desk, reservations, catering, sales, and general manager. I spent over five years as the corporate director of sales and marketing for the company and oversaw a revenue budget of $80 million for 15 hotels in the DC area.


Milestone Internet Marketing - I helped hotels develop their websites, helped them with their analytics, paid marketing campaigns and general internet marketing education.


Signature Worldwide - I helped several hundred hotels increase sales by improving the performance of their employees. Most recently I designed a sales training program called Stand Apart Sales that has been well received. See the website I created for the class here. Stand Apart Sales
